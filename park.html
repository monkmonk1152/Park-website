History Of The Park

The Park is named after the Moon people, who lived in these woods for thouands of years. They where night people, hunting and moving at night, they had big eyes and very pale skin. They were also described as being little in stature, the men bearded, light- big eyed, and having pale white skin. Cherokee Indians told stories of these people and talked of a King known as MaDog. Some people belivie they where welsh decents but noone knows for sure. They left many ruines behind, along with many tales. They lived in the vast of these amazing woods and mountains, you can see for yourself as you walk the trails and explore

Activities At The Park

There are many things to do in the park for all people, of all ages! There is family activies like swimming, fishing, and camp fires. Our guest can enjoy all the trails and hiking, along with site seeing and learning the history of the woods, You can camp in a tent or enjoy a nice cozy cabin, there is many animals to see, with miles of trails. We have plenty of woods to explore and we are dog friendly park.
